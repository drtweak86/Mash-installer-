## 🎯 STRATEGIC RATIONALE

1. **Shaft U (The Foundation)**: We purify the ore *first*. Hardening the codebase ensures that every subsequent feature is built on a stable, idiomatic, and secure foundation.

2. **Shafts S & T (The Intelligence)**: "You cannot guide what you do not see." Profiling the system (S) and creating an advice engine (T) provides the intelligence needed to optimize the installation flow (I) and suggest the right aesthetic presets (W).

3. **Shaft I (The Heart)**: With a hardened and intelligent core, we deliver the actual software catalog and core installation modes.

4. **Shaft V (The Connection)**: We move beyond binary installation to fully configured environments through interactive setup and authorizations.

5. **Shaft W (The Polish)**: The final layer of beauty. High-quality themes and dotfiles are the "gleam on the blade" that makes the MASH experience complete.
